About Me

I have a background in Graphic Arts and Web Design & Development, but my true passion lies in Generative AI and the broader AI landscape. I enjoy working with AI-driven tools, whether for web development, digital art, podcast creation, or continuous learning. I have created hundreds of AI-generated artworks using platforms like Kaiber and MidJourney and showcase my work on Instagram. In 2022, I wrote an anime-focused podcast, leveraging ChatGPT to assist in content creation. Through extensive research on the history of anime, I not only deepened my knowledge

of the subject but also honed my skills in crafting effective Generative AI prompts to organize and structure information. As my interest in AI has grown, I have earned certifications in AI prompt engineering and large language model (LLM) fundamentals. Recently, I joined Kaggle to engage with a diverse community of AI enthusiasts, from beginners to professionals, and to further develop my skills. Whether through freelancing or working with an organization, I am committed to building a career in AI.
